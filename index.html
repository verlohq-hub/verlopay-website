Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is Verlo a marketplace? ↓
No. Verlo doesn't list sellers or take over your customers. You stay in control — we just handle the payment protection.
Who controls when funds are released? ↓
You do, with buyer protection built in. Once you mark an order as delivered, the buyer has 72 hours to raise any issues. No dispute? Funds release automatically. No chasing payments.
What if a buyer falsely claims non-delivery? ↓
Sellers are protected too. You can upload proof — screenshots of work sent, chat logs, or tracking numbers for physical items. False disputes don't automatically win. Evidence matters.
How much does Verlo cost? ↓
Verlo charges a small fee per transaction (typically 2–3%) to cover escrow, fraud protection, and dispute handling. Early waitlist members get priority access, special launch pricing, and direct support from the founding team.
How long do disputes take? ↓
Usually 24–48 hours. We don't leave either side stuck waiting.
When is Verlo launching? ↓
Early access will roll out in phases with a small group of sellers first. Join the waitlist to be notified when access opens for you.
Will this work outside the UK? ↓
We're starting in the UK and expanding from there. Join the waitlist and we'll let you know when it's available in your region.
