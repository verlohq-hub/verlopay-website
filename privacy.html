Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025  ·  Verlo, United Kingdom

1 Introduction

Verlo ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website or join the Verlo waitlist.

2 Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal information: name, email address, country/location
  • Usage information: basic analytics such as page visits and interactions
  • Communication data: emails or messages you send to us
Note: We do not collect payment details at the waitlist stage.

2.5 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Understand how visitors use our site (e.g. via Google Analytics or similar tools)
  • Improve site performance and user experience

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Add you to the Verlo waitlist
  • Send product updates and early access notifications
  • Communicate important service-related information
  • Understand demand and improve our product
We will never sell your data to third parties.

4 Legal Basis for Processing (UK & EU GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent — when you join the waitlist
  • Legitimate interest — to communicate updates and improve our service

You may withdraw consent at any time.

5 Email Communications

By joining the waitlist, you agree to receive emails from Verlo about:

  • Early access
  • Product updates
  • Important announcements

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails.

6 Data Storage, Security & Retention

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

Data Retention — We retain your waitlist information until:

  • Verlo launches and you become a user, or
  • You request deletion, or
  • 24 months of inactivity — whichever occurs first
When Verlo introduces payment services, funds will be held in secure, regulated accounts and never mixed with company funds.

7 Data Sharing

We may share data with trusted service providers, including:

  • Email platforms (e.g. Loops, ConvertKit) to send updates
  • Analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics, Plausible) to understand site usage
  • Hosting providers (e.g. Netlify, Webflow, Vercel) to operate the website

All providers are GDPR-compliant and process data only according to our instructions.

8 International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred outside the UK or EU, appropriate safeguards are used to ensure GDPR compliance.

9 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

9.5 Children's Privacy

Verlo is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
